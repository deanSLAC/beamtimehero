# BL15-2 Experiment modes

How to determine the experiment type running based on the counters available

Most often, the beamline will be running HERFD, in which case we use the counters vortDT and vortDT2 primarily.  

XRS: Sometimes when we are using vortDT it is actually to do x-ray Raman spectroscopy instead of standard xas. This will be apparent based on the energies we are looking at so you would need to look beyond the counters to the actual energy range being scanned to be sure.  Our raman spectrometers have an energy of ~6462, so to look at carbon we go ~300 eV above that, to 6700eV. This is to use the Si440 reflection, if we use 660 reflection multiply that 6462 by 1.5 to get 9693eV. 

If the counter ppboff is present, we are doing time resolved measurements (uv-vis pump, x-ray probe)

If the counter mod0 is present, we are doing modulation excitation spectroscopy (MES XAS) and the important counters are mod0-mod11.
